# Color Accent Review

Date: 2026-03-15

## What feels visually flat now

- Too many secondary surfaces were using the same near-neutral border and fill values.
- Small labels carried most of the brand color while higher-value UI moments stayed muted.
- Hover states were often functional, but not memorable.
- Status pills, tags, and section markers were readable but not very distinctive.

## Where accent color should become stronger

- Secondary CTAs and recruiter-path buttons.
- Pills, tags, and status markers.
- Section labels and small dot markers.
- Hover borders on cards and linked proof surfaces.
- The brand mark and a few selected warm-gradient surfaces.

## What should remain neutral

- Main body text.
- Primary headlines.
- Large background fields.
- Most content cards outside their small accent moments.
- The primary black CTA, which still works well as the strongest action.

## What exact UI areas should carry more brand energy

- Hero top pill and secondary CTA.
- Hero signal cards and right-panel badge.
- Project section marker and project-card hover borders.
- Tech badges, readiness chips, and stage / visibility pills.
- Poker vertical card as a controlled brand moment.
- About status pill and recruiter-path buttons.
- Navbar badge, nav hover states, and BrandMark surface.

## Open decisions still needing Chris

- Whether the warm bronze accent should become slightly more visible in the hero itself.
- Whether the primary CTA should remain pure black or pick up a subtle brand-edge treatment later.
- Whether archive/ops cards should stay mostly neutral or get a slightly stronger accent treatment too.
