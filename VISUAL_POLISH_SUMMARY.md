# Visual Polish Summary

Date: 2026-03-15

## What changed

- Reduced text heaviness in the hero, featured cards, homepage section intros, and `/about`.
- Tightened supporting card copy and line-height so key panels read faster.
- Swapped heavier numbered lists for lighter accent-dot lists in the hero support panels.
- Strengthened warm brand accent usage in pills, secondary CTAs, hover borders, status markers, and small section labels.
- Kept large surfaces, body text, and primary headlines mostly neutral.

## Why it helps

- The homepage and `/about` now have a faster visual read and clearer focal points.
- Smaller text blocks create more breathing room without changing the site structure.
- Accent color now lands in the places that carry identity and action, instead of disappearing into the overall neutral system.
- The result is cleaner, calmer, and more memorable without becoming flashy.

## What stayed the same

- The site structure.
- The minimal brand language.
- The recruiter-first positioning.
- The overall warm neutral palette and restrained feel.

## Open decisions still needing Chris

- Whether the hero should get one more reduction pass on supporting text.
- Whether the right-side hero panel should stay as a support card or become even more compact later.
- Whether the bronze accent level is exactly right now, or should be dialed slightly up or down.
