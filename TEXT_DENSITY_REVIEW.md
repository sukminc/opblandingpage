# Text Density Review

Date: 2026-03-15

## What was reduced

- Removed one full supporting paragraph from the homepage hero.
- Shortened hero signal cards and principle lines.
- Tightened homepage section descriptions in Projects and About.
- Removed the extra body paragraph from featured product cards.
- Reduced poker section copy to a single short explanatory line.
- Tightened `/about` paragraphs so the page reads faster.
- Kept the `/about` support cards compact and one-line.

## What was kept

- The main hiring/trust story.
- The strongest hero headline.
- The current build / philosophy / credibility order on `/about`.
- The project structure and recruiter-fast navigation.
- The core brand voice: calm, direct, intentional.

## Why the cuts improve the visual feel

- Less copy creates more breathing room around the existing layout.
- Shorter blocks make hierarchy clearer and improve first-scan speed.
- Cards feel lighter when they carry one idea instead of a mini-explanation.
- The site reads more premium when it trusts the strongest lines to do the work.
- Recruiters can parse the page faster without losing the core story.

## Open decisions still needing Chris

- Whether the hero should get even leaner and rely more on the signal cards.
- Whether the right-side hero panel still needs all three principle lines.
- Whether `/about` should keep three short intro paragraphs or compress to two.
